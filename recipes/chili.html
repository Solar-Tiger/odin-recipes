Homemade Chili

Nothing like a heartwarming chili, now is there? While many recipes try to imitate chili, this recipe was born in it, raised in it. This tried & true chili recipe will have you coming back for seconds, for thirds, for forths even!

This chili recipe is so easy to make, you can leave it alone for 45 minutes and come back to a nice hot meal, and you quite literally do that with this recipe! Give it time to simmer and absorb those juices and you'll never make another chili recipe again

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • Salt/pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, diced
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons worcestershire
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 28 oz. crushed tomatoes
  • 14.5 oz. diced tomatoes
  • 16 oz. kidney beans

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Add the onions and let them soften, about 8 minutes
  3. Season the gronud beef with salt and pepper then increase heat to medium-high
  4. Add the ground beef and cook till completely brown, about 5 minutes
  5. Add the garlic and bell/jalapeno peppers and cook for 3 minutes
  6. Drain excess grease then add the seasonings (chili powder - red pepper flakes) and mix well
  7. Add the tomato paste, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce and hot sauce, continuing to mix well
  8. Add the chicken broth, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pot
  9. Add the beef bouillon, crushed tomatoes and diced tomatoes and bring to a boil before reducing it to a simmer
  10. Cover partially and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally to lift any meat that has settled at the bottom
  11. Stir in the drained kidney beans after this and simmer for 15 more minutes
  12. Taste and season as needed before letting it rest for 5 minutes
  13. Serve with any garnishings of your choices (shredded cheese, sour cream, etc.)
