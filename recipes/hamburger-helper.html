Homemade Hamburger Helper

On cold winter evenings no one wants to slave over an oven to make a hearthy meal. That's where this recipe comes into play! Whether you're Homemade cheeseburger hamburger helper is a quick, easy and delicious recipe everyone in the family will love and help keep them warm on chilly, winter evenings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta (any kind works, macoroni recommended)
  • 1 pound ground Beef
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium high heat and brown ground beef
  2. Add cornstarch, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, sugar and pepper and mix well
  3. Add hot water, milk and pasta
  4. Bring to a boil then cover and let simmer for 8-11 minutes
  5. Sprinkle cheese over the cooked pasta until melted
  6. Let stand for a few minutes so sauce thickens then server
